Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Portl Share ("Application") for mobile devices, which was created by Karyanest Softwares.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Portl Share is a completely local, offline-first application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or device-specific identifiers. All file transfers occur directly between the devices you choose to connect over your own local network (Wi-Fi or hotspot).

2. System Permissions Required

To perform local file transfers, the Application requires specific permissions on your device. These permissions are used exclusively to enable the core file-transfer features locally:

  • Local Network / Wi-Fi Access: Required to scan for, establish a direct connection to, and transfer files with other devices on your local network. No external internet servers are used for data transmission.
  • Storage / Files Access: Required to read files you choose to share from your device, and to write/save incoming files received from paired devices.
  • Location / Bluetooth Access (Android older versions): Required by the Android OS to discover nearby Wi-Fi networks and local devices. Portl Share does not track or store your location.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application does not contain any third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising frameworks, or cloud storage integrations. Your data remains entirely under your control.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly solicit or collect data from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us at Karyanest@gmail.com.
