Bluetooth Connection Setup

Windows Host Pairing
To connect Onelink Remote to a Windows PC:
  1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your Windows PC.
  2. Open Onelink Remote on your Android device and tap **"Advertise / Start Pairing"**.
  3. On Windows, navigate to **Settings > Bluetooth & Devices > Add Device > Bluetooth**.
  4. Select your device from the discovered nodes. Once paired, Windows will recognize the Android host as a standard Bluetooth Keyboard/Mouse.
macOS Host Pairing
To connect to your Mac:
  1. Open **System Settings > Bluetooth** on your Mac.
  2. Launch Onelink Remote on your phone and activate the advertisement beacon.
  3. Under the Mac Bluetooth discovery panel, click **Connect** next to your Android device name.
  4. Grant keyboard simulation approval if prompted by macOS security settings.
Android TV / Smart TV Pairing
To connect to a Smart TV:
  1. Open your Smart TV or Android TV settings and go to **Remotes & Accessories > Add Accessory**.
  2. Launch the Onelink Remote pairing mode.
  3. Select the controller from the list on your TV screen. You can now use the virtual trackpad/dpad to navigate your TV menus.
